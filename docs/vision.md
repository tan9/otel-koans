# Vision

Teach OpenTelemetry through hands-on, progressive exercises inspired by the Ruby Koans pattern.

## Goals

1. A learner with no prior OTel knowledge can work through the exercises and gain practical understanding
2. Exercises teach through doing — filling in blanks, fixing failing tests, building small programs
3. Progressive difficulty with clear ordering
4. Technically accurate — all content aligns with OTel specifications
5. Language and tooling decisions are TBD — the content design comes first
